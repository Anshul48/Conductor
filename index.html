An Ecosystem, Not Just an App

What we've described is just the core. Conductor is designed as a powerful, lean foundation for an entire ecosystem of intelligence.

True Modularity

Its real power unlocks through modules. Imagine a "Study Tutor" module that analyzes past exam questions, a "PhD Researcher" module that assists with literature reviews, or a "Content Creator" module that helps draft articles from your notes. The platform is built for extension.

Augment, Don't Replace

This philosophy extends to your tools. We believe in augmenting the workflows you already love. That's why we're starting with seamless, 100% backwards-compatible integration for Obsidian. You don't migrate to Conductor; you empower your existing vault with Conductor.
